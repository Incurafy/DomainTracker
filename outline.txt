# What the actual fuck is this?
- A way to track "domains" and their intrigues, settlements, resources, units, and armies.

# Domains
- Engage in intrigue
- Spy on other domains
- Control settlements
- Control strongholds
- Recruit units
- Raise armies
- Command armies

# Settlements
- Generate resources
- Are the source of units

# Resources
- Are used to recruit, train, and equip units
- Are used to build and upgrade strongholds

# Units
- Form armies
- Garrison strongholds
- Defend settlements

# Armies
- Consist of units
- March across the map
- Battle other armies
- Besiege settlements

# Strongholds
- Can be in a settlement
- Can be alone without a settlement
- Can be garrisoned by units
